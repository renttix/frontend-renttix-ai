Navigation Path

To access the Document Appearance system:

  1. Click on the hamburger menu (☰) in the top-left corner
  2. Scroll down to find "System Setup" in the sidebar
  3. Click on "System Setup"
  4. Find and click on the "Document Appearance" tile
