How it works:

  1. Select a product - Check the box to add it to your order
  2. Set quantity - Use the +/- buttons or type the amount needed
  3. Enable maintenance - Check the maintenance box if the product needs servicing
  4. Configure schedule - Smart defaults are applied based on product type:
    • Toilets: Every 7 days (weekly cleaning)
    • Generators: Every 30 days (monthly inspection)
    • Pumps/Compressors: Every 14 days
    • Other products: Every 30 days (default)
  5. Advanced options - Click "Advanced" for additional settings like frequency presets and service duration
